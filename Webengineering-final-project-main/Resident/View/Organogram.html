Municipality or municipality is an important unit of Bangladesh and the urban autonomous state of West Bengal state of India. The name of municipality and West Bengal name is common in Bangladesh. The number of total municipalities in Bangladesh is 326 [1] and in West Bengal, the number of municipalities is 119.


History:

The first law was passed in 1842 to establish municipal corporation. According to the Bengal Municipality Act or the Bengal Municipal Act introduced in undivided Bengal in 1932 and the provisions of 1980, 1982 and 1992, the municipal wings of West Bengal were managed. West Bengal municipal law or the West Bengal Municipal Act was introduced in 1993 for the purpose of conducting municipal elections in West Bengal. The amendment of this law was made in 1994 and 1995. At present, the West Bengal Government is established under the provisions of this Act.Dhaka Municipality was formed in 1864. The municipality ordinance was issued in independent Bangladesh in 1977. According to the Local Government (Municipality) Act of Bangladesh, 2009, municipalities of Bangladesh established and operated.


According to the local government (municipality) law, 2009 municipalities are divided into 3 categories. These are:

  1. 'A' Class
  2. 'B' Class
  3. 'C' Category [2]
